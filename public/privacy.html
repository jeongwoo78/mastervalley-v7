3. Photo Data and AI Processing

When you use the App's photo transformation feature, your photo is transmitted to third-party AI services for real-time processing. We collect and transmit only the minimum data necessary for the requested transformation (the photo itself and selected style parameters). This is how your photo data is handled:

Your photo is sent to Replicate (for AI image generation using the FLUX model), Anthropic (for photo analysis and art style matching), Google (for image editing via the Gemini model when you request modifications), and OpenAI (for master artist dialogue and GPT-4o vision-based commentary on transformed images). These services process your photo solely to generate your requested transformation and related features. We do not intentionally retain your photos beyond the time necessary for real-time processing; however, transient caching may occur for technical or security purposes. Third-party retention is governed by each provider's own data retention policies. We do not extract, collect, store, or use facial geometry, biometric identifiers, or biometric information from your photos, because we do not perform facial landmark detection, embedding generation, or any form of biometric template creation. Your photos are not used to train AI models, build identification databases, or develop facial recognition technology. We implement prompt-level restrictions, output scanning, and safety classifiers to minimize the risk of generating non-consensual intimate imagery or harmful content.

4. Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect location data, contacts, browsing history, or device sensor data. We do not use advertising trackers or sell your data to third parties. We do not create or store biometric profiles or face templates from your photos.

5. Local Storage

Transformation results saved to your gallery are stored locally on your device using IndexedDB. This data never leaves your device unless you choose to share or export it. Clearing your browser data or uninstalling the App may remove locally stored gallery data.

All transformation outputs include embedded metadata (EXIF tags) indicating that the image was generated by AI, in compliance with applicable AI transparency regulations. This metadata identifies the image as "AI Generated by Master Valley" and cannot be removed through normal use of the App.

7. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to: provide and maintain the App's functionality; process your photo transformations; manage your credit balance and purchases; communicate with you about your account or support requests; and improve the App's performance and user experience.

8. Data Retention

Account information and transaction data are retained as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your data from our systems within 30 days, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes (such as fraud prevention or financial record-keeping). Photo data is not intentionally retained beyond real-time processing. Transient caching may occur for technical or security purposes, and third-party service providers may retain data in accordance with their own policies.

9. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS), secure authentication via Firebase, and server-side access controls. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 18 and older. Users under 18 may not create accounts or use the App independently. When a photo containing a minor is uploaded by an authorized adult user (such as a parent or legal guardian), our AI system automatically applies enhanced safety measures designed to promote age-appropriate processing and minimize the risk of sexual, nude, or inappropriate content being generated. However, no AI system can guarantee absolute prevention in all cases. Personal information of minors is not collected, stored, or used beyond what is strictly necessary for real-time photo transformation processing. If you believe a minor's data has been improperly collected or processed, please contact us immediately at support@mastervalley.app.
